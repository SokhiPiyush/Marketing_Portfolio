Understanding SWOT Analysis

SWOT Analysis is a strategic planning technique used to identify Strengths, Weaknesses, Opportunities, and Threats related to business competition or project planning.

It helps businesses leverage their strengths, improve weaknesses, seize opportunities, and mitigate risks to stay competitive.

SWOT Analysis Components

Strengths

Internal factors that give a competitive edge, such as strong brand identity, high customer loyalty, and superior technology.

Weaknesses

Internal challenges that need improvement, such as high production costs, outdated technology, or lack of skilled workforce.

Opportunities

External factors that a business can leverage, such as market growth, technological advancements, or changing consumer preferences.

Threats

External risks that could impact business performance, such as economic downturns, regulatory changes, or emerging competitors.
